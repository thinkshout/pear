ThinkShout's Summer Mission

ThinkShout believes in creating lasting partnerships with nonprofits and forward thinking companies. Participating in our campaign is more than just buying a t-shirt. All of your money will go directly to p:ear, to fund programs like their Bike Mechanic and Barista schools which will help students learn lifelong skills and have a chance for more equal opportunities. Your money does more than just fund programs. $28 can buy three students breakfast and dinner. $25 can help with the cost of bus tickets and a pair of socks, journals and pens, sketchbook and pastels, first aid kits and even a hoodie.
